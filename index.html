An interaction developer | designer based in Toronto, Canada.
Investigates interaction between user and the goal using website.

My interest is in application of technologies such as NFC, AR and VR in real life.

After graduating University of Toronto, I took Interactive Media Management program at George Brown College where I learned how to design interactive media. By combining my development skills I learned from University and Bitmaker Lab, I was able to create websites of my own.

I strongly think that the future of the website will not simply be in the monitor nor cell phone which is why I am highly interested in how to apply technologies such as near-field communication, augmented reality and virtual reality into modern website experiences.
